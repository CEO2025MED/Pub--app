# Publinx: Artist Publishing Hub

A centralized dashboard for music artists to access all necessary publishing, royalty, and registration services. Features an autofill helper to streamline repetitive form submissions.

## Features
- **Centralized Dashboard**: Access all your essential music publishing links in one place.
- **Autofill Helper**: Save your common artist information (PRO numbers, IPI, publisher info) to quickly copy-paste into registration forms.
- **Categorized Links**: Services are organized by category (PROs, Distributors, SoundExchange, etc.).
- **Drag & Drop Reordering**: Customize the order of your links to suit your workflow.
- **Search & Filter**: Quickly find the specific service you need.

## Tech Stack
- React 19
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Vite
